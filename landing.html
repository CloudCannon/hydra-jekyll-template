Keen to come to PowerUp but want more info?


PowerUp is a practical training conference that will skill up 1000 everyday people to take down the Adani coal mine and other injustices through local community organising. PowerUp is the landmark national training conference for GetUp — Australia’s largest campaigning community of over one-million members.

What: PowerUp Training Conference
When: Saturday 23 - Sunday 24 September
Where: UNSW, Sydney
Tickets: $70 concession, $90 full-priced

At PowerUp, you'll develop practical organising skills and grassroots strategy in engaging, interactive workshops, and be inspired by some of Australia and the world's most preeminent community organisers like Sally McManus (ACTU Secretary), Becky Bond (Bernie Sanders Election Campaign Advisor) and more.

Most importantly, you'll build lasting relationships with locals from your home electorate and gain a supportive network to take action with moving forward.
