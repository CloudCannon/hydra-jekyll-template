We help investors make informed decisions by providing real time, data-driven, market projections.

Our customers are fund managers and options traders looking for an additional edge when making investment decisions. The solutions we offer at CMidas Financial can give you better insight into:

  • Current price, volatility, and volume trends with confidence intervals for multiple symbols traded at Nasdaq.
  • Option trading opportunities with high probability of success, given the current market scenario.
  • Medium and long-term macroeconomic projections for the United States economy generated by our data-driven models.

We are transforming the financial industry by bringing to our customers state-of-the-art artificial intelligence algorithms previously available only to research groups. This combination of research knowledge and real world expertise in quantitative trading set us apart from our competition.

Our principles:


Focus on the end result: the alpha.

Every data sample we process, every line of code we write, every decision we make, it all has a single purpose: to make well informed investment decisions that generate profit, excess returns, alpha.

Data-driven decision-making.

Data speaks, and we listen. Combining state-of-the-art artificial intelligence algorithms to classical statistical modeling, our data-driven solutions process real world market data in order to suggest potential trading opportunities.

Precise and timely execution.

Financial systems are not static, neither are our models. All our financial modeling and quantitative analysis are done in real-time, with real world financial data, in specialized computer hardware. We believe accurate modeling is nothing without timely execution.
