Join us at PowerUp!

Join us at PowerUp, GetUp’s first national conference in Sydney on 23-24 September.


At PowerUp, you will join one thousand passionate GetUp members for a weekend of skills workshops, relationship-building and strategy sessions. Together, we’ll launch a national network of GetUp Action Groups and make a plan to retake power for everyday people.

If you want to get the skills, tools and connections to make a difference and you’re ready to take action, then we need you at PowerUp.

PowerUp is held at the University of NSW in Sydney. Spaces are limited and will sell-out, so please register your interest now to avoid missing out.
