Questions?

Ticket

What does my ticket include?

Your ticket covers you for the entire two-day conference, plus tea and coffee in the morning and afternoon breaks, and light snacks. Your ticket also serves as a ticket to the PowerUp after-party on Saturday evening.

What doesn’t my ticket include?

To keep the cost of tickets down, lunch is not included. Instead we’ve organised for the nearby UNSW foodcourt to remain open so you have a great choice of lunch on both days.
